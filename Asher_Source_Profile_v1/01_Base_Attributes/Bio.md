# Bio

## 中文

**Asher Zhang (修荷)**

一人公司创始人，《蜕升》作者。

专注于 AI 时代的价值交付与个体成长——研究当 AI 能做一切时，人的不可替代性在哪里。构建了"修荷系统"：一套将个人认知外化、结构化存储的能力操作系统，让 AI 成为思维外骨骼而非替代品。核心产品 Asyre（非对称内容创作系统）通过意图驱动架构，帮助创作者用 AI 放大独特价值，而非被 AI 同质化。

相信飞升不是变强，是成为能帮助别人变强的人。

## English

**Asher Zhang (修荷)**

Solo founder. Author of *Ascension (蜕升)*.

Focused on value delivery and individual growth in the AI era — exploring what makes humans irreplaceable when AI can do everything. Built the Xiuhe System: a personal cognitive operating system that externalizes thinking into structured, retrievable knowledge, turning AI into an exoskeleton for the mind rather than a replacement. Core product Asyre (Asymmetric Content Creation System) uses intent-driven architecture to help creators amplify their unique value with AI instead of being homogenized by it.

Believes that true ascension is not becoming powerful — it's becoming someone who empowers others.
